# Product Overview

Athletic Academics Hub (AAH) is a SaaS platform for university athletic academic support programs, with emphasis on NCAA Division I institutions. The platform helps student-athletes balance athletic commitments with educational requirements while ensuring NCAA eligibility compliance.

## Core Purpose

Enable academic staff, coaches, faculty, and student-athletes to manage the intersection of athletics and academics through digitized support programs. The platform ensures student-athletes maintain NCAA eligibility, progress toward degrees, and develop essential life skills.

## Key Capabilities

- **Academic Support**: Advising, tutoring, study hall tracking, and progress monitoring
- **NCAA Compliance**: Automated Division I eligibility tracking (GPA, credit hours, progress-toward-degree)
- **AI-Powered Assistance**: 24/7 conversational AI, intelligent advising, predictive analytics, and automated workflows
- **Life Skills Development**: Workshops, career development, mental health resources, and peer mentoring
- **Faculty Coordination**: Travel letters, absence notifications, and progress report management
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Dashboards for performance visualization and compliance tracking

## Target Users

- **Student-Athletes**: Access schedules, resources, track progress, check eligibility status
- **Academic Support Staff**: Manage programs, monitor progress, generate reports, coordinate services
- **Coaches**: View team academic performance, ensure compliance, request accommodations
- **Faculty**: Submit progress reports, communicate absences, coordinate accommodations
- **Mentors/Peers**: Facilitate peer support sessions

## Business Goals

- Enhance student-athlete academic success with 90% eligibility compliance
- Reduce administrative workload by 60% through AI automation
- Provide 24/7 intelligent support with sub-second response times
- Increase early intervention effectiveness by 40% through predictive analytics
- Scale support quality without proportional staff increases
- Differentiate through advanced AI capabilities while maintaining NCAA compliance
